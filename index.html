Conceptual Model

Entities & Attributes

profile

  • profileActivationToken
  • profileCohort
  • profileEmail

idea

  • ideaId (PK)
  • ideaProfileId (FK)
  • ideaArchived
  • ideaDescription
  • ideaName

vote

  • voteIdeaId (FK)
  • voteProfileId (FK)

rank

  • rankIdeaId (FK)
  • rankProfileId (FK)
  • rankValue

Relations

  • One profile can vote many times (1 to many)
  • A profile can also rank multiple times, as well (1 to many)
  • An idea can be voted on many times (1 to many)
  • In addition to it (the single idea) being ranked several times (1 to many)
